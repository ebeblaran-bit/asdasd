🎟️ How to Book Tickets
1

Browse Movies

Go to the Movies page to see what's currently showing and coming soon at cinemas near you.

2

Choose a Schedule

Select your preferred cinema, date, and showtime. Available seats are shown in real time.

3

Pick Your Seats

Click available seats on the interactive seat map. Green = available, Yellow = VIP, Red = taken. Max 10 seats per booking.

4

Enter Details & Pay

Enter your name, contact number, and choose your ticket type (Regular / Student / Senior/PWD). Proceed to payment via GCash, Maya, or card.

5

Receive Your Booking

After payment, you'll get a booking reference code. Show this at the cinema counter to claim your tickets. Arrive 15 minutes early!

🎬 Booking Questions
Once a booking is confirmed and paid, cancellations must be requested at the cinema counter. Online cancellation is currently not supported. Seats are non-transferable.
You can book up to 10 seats per transaction. If you need more, please make a second booking.
Seats are locked for 15 minutes from the time you select them. If payment is not completed within that time, the seats are released back to the public.
Select "Student", or "Senior/PWD" as your ticket type during booking. The discount will be automatically applied to your booking total.
Click your profile picture in the top-left corner and select "My Bookings", or go to My Bookings directly. All your past and upcoming bookings are listed there.
💳 Payment Questions
On the payment page, select GCash. A QR code will appear — open your GCash app, tap "Pay QR", and scan the code. The amount will be deducted from your GCash wallet automatically. Your booking is confirmed once payment is verified.
Yes! Select Maya on the payment page. You'll be redirected to the Maya app or website to complete the payment. Return to TICK.IT after payment to see your confirmation.
We accept Visa and Mastercard credit and debit cards. Card details are processed securely through PayMongo — TICK.IT never stores your card information.
If payment fails, your seats will be automatically released. You can try booking again with a different payment method. Make sure your GCash/Maya wallet has sufficient balance, or check with your card issuer if using a card.
This is rare but can happen due to network issues. Please contact us immediately with your payment proof (GCash/Maya receipt or card statement) and we will resolve it within 24 hours.
📍 Cinema Locations
We currently operate at: SM Seaside Cebu (SRP), Gaisano Grand Minglanilla, Nustar Cebu Cinema (SRP), Cebu IL CORSO Cinema (South Road Properties), UC Cantao-an (Naga, Cebu), and TOPS Cebu Skydom (Busay).
VIP seats (shown in gold/yellow on the seat map) are premium seats in the prime viewing area, typically rows A–B at the front of the premium section. They are priced the same as Regular seats (₱450). The Admin can customize seat layouts per hall.
We recommend arriving at least 15 minutes before the showtime. Bring your booking reference code (or screenshot) to show at the counter.
